\section{微分方程}
The general solution to the linear homogeneous second-order differential equation:
\begin{equation}
	y'' + py' + qy = 0
\end{equation}
is $y = c_1 e^{s_1 x} + c_2 e^{s_2 x}$ where $s_1$ and $s_2$ are roots of the auxiliary equation $s^2 + ps + q = 0$.
